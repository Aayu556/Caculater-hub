What is the 50-20-20-10 Rule?

The 50-20-20-10 budget rule helps you allocate your monthly salary wisely: 50% for Essentials (rent, food, utilities), 20% for Savings (emergency fund), 20% for Investments (SIP, stocks, mutual funds), and 10% for Luxury (entertainment, travel, dining out).

How to use this Smart Salary Calculator?

Enter your monthly take-home salary in the input field. The calculator instantly shows how much you should spend in each category. You can also edit the percentages to match your personal budget goals.
